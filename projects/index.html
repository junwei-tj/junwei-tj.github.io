STARS Panel

STARS Panel is a course registration planner for NTU students. It aims to make timetable planning easy for students with features such as automatic timetable generation with special filters, as well as the editing and saving of timetable plans. Advanced options, such as eliminate back-to-back lessons, allow students to better plan their ideal timetables.
